About Us

We are a youth organization named ASURA. We conduct poojas and festivals such as Rath Yatra, Saraswati Pooja, Durga Pooja, and many others. We also donate to needy children. Our motto is 'Sewa Paramo Dharma,' which means 'Service is our prime duty.'
